How To Play:

There are two betting options. You may bet minimum (10 coins) and get a +200 bonus if you win. Or you may bet max (500 coins) and get a +2000 bonus if you win. If you hit a jackpot (all 3 reels match) you get a +50,000 bonus. These are probably not the official rules but this is all I could gather from reading online.
